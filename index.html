I am an Associate Professor in Data Sceince at Department of Applied Data Science,Noroff Univeristy College, Kristiansand (Norway) since September 2018 and part of the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. Since 2019, I have served as the Program Leader for Applied Data Science bachelor degree program, managing and coordinating the learning activities of over one-hundred students several teaching staff. I am also a senior data science research consultant specializing in statistical data analysis, machine learning applications in business analytics, and design of decision support systems. Betweem 2019 to 2024, I worked as a research consultant on European Economic Area-funded project under the 2021-2024 grant ( Project Number: GG-PDP1-001 ) for the Department for Strategic Development and Coordination of Public Administration, Ministry of the Interior of the Czech Republic. The project analyzed citizens' mobility from mobile positioning data to plan and distribute public services and administration efficiently in the Czech Republic. I provided technical guidance for adapting big data analytic processes and best practices to analyze mobile positioning data. Previously, I worked as assistant Professor in Computer Science and Engineering at Jouf University where I contributed to the development of teaching and research activities. Before joining Jouf University in 2016, I worked as a Lecturer in Artificial Intelligence at the Faculty of Computing Federal University Dutse (Nigeria) where I contributed to the teaching and research activities.

My research interests developing computer vision and AI techniques for context awareness, machine consciousness and navigation in minimally invasive and robot-assisted surgery. Particularly, I am interested in context understanding in endoscopic procedures, surgical workflow analysis, field-of-view expansion, 3D reconstruction, perception and navigation for surgical robotics.

I received the BEng in Electrical Electronics Engineering from Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University, Bauchi, (Nigeria). In 2007, I was awarded a Research Assistant Fellowship for the MSc in Computer Engineering by King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Dhahran, (KSA). In 2015, I received a joint PhD from Queen Mary University of London (UK) and University of Genoa (Italy), funded by an Erasmus Mundus Fellowship.
I am member of professional bodies including Institute for Electrical and Electronic Engineers (IEEE) and IEEE-Computer Society .

Areas of Interest

Data Science, Smart Systems, Human-activity Monitoring, Computer Vision, Computer-assisted Diagnosis.
